Trip. by Feather Studio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Effective immediately

Your privacy matters. Trip. is designed from the ground up to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

1. Data Collection

Trip. does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device. No account creation is required, and no data is transmitted to our servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Trip names, destinations, and dates
  • Places, expenses, and journal entries
  • Photos added to trips and places
  • App settings and preferences

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync in settings, your trip data will be stored in your personal iCloud account and synced across your Apple devices. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Feather Studio does not have access to your iCloud data.

3. Photos

Photos you add through Trip. are saved to your device's local storage and, when taken through the camera, also saved to your native Photos library. Photos are never uploaded to any external server.

4. Third-Party Services

Trip. uses the following third-party services to provide certain features. These services may collect limited data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • OpenWeather API — used to fetch weather forecasts for trip destinations. Your destination city name is sent to OpenWeather to retrieve weather data. See OpenWeather's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash — used to fetch cover images for trips. Search queries (destination names) are sent to Unsplash. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy.
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — used to geocode destination names into coordinates. See OSM's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo — used to fetch historical climate data for destinations. See Open-Meteo's Terms.

5. Analytics & Advertising

Trip. does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Trip. is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Profile → Danger Zone → Delete all data. This permanently removes all trips, places, expenses, photos, and settings from the app. To remove iCloud data, delete the app or manage your iCloud storage through iOS Settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at featherstudio@proton.me.
