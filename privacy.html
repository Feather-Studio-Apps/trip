Trip.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective immediately  ·  Developer: Daren Irlen

Trip. is designed from the ground up to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. This policy explains exactly what data is handled and how.
1. Data Collection

What we collect

Trip. does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device. No account creation is required, and no data is transmitted to servers operated by Daren Irlen or Feather Studio.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Trip names, destinations, and dates
  • Places, expenses, and budget information
  • Journal entries and photo captions
  • Photos added to trips and places
  • Prep checklists and packing lists
  • Companion names
  • App settings and preferences
2. iCloud Sync

Optional cloud backup

If you enable iCloud Sync in Profile → Preferences, your trip data will be stored in your personal iCloud account and synced across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit service.

This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Daren Irlen and Feather Studio do not have access to your iCloud data at any time.

App preferences, appearance settings, and notification preferences are not synced via iCloud and remain local to each device.

3. Photos

Photo handling

Photos you add through Trip. are saved to your device's local storage and are never uploaded to any external server. When you take a photo through the app's camera feature, it is also saved to your native Photos library as a backup.

Photos are not included in the trip export/import feature as they are stored locally and cannot be transferred between devices this way.

4. Third-Party Services

External services used

Trip. uses the following third-party services to provide certain features. These services receive limited, non-personal data as described:

  • Unsplash — used to search and display cover photos for trips. Your search query (typically a destination name) is sent to Unsplash. No personal information is transmitted. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy.
  • Nominatim / OpenStreetMap — used to search for places and geocode destination names. Your search query is sent to the Nominatim public API. No personal information is transmitted. See OSM's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenWeather API — used to display current weather for active trip destinations. Your destination city name is sent to OpenWeather. No personal information is transmitted. See OpenWeather's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple CloudKit — optional iCloud sync. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
5. Analytics & Advertising

No tracking

Trip. does not use any analytics, crash reporting, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track how you use the app, how often you open it, or what features you use.

Trip. products are ad-free. No advertising is served within the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Age restrictions

Trip. is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it.

7. Data Deletion

Your right to delete

You can delete all your data at any time from Profile → Danger Zone → Delete all data. This permanently removes all trips, places, expenses, photos, and settings from the app on your device.

To remove data synced to iCloud, delete the app or manage your iCloud storage through iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

Photos saved to your native Photos app are not affected by in-app data deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

Policy updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time as the app evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
