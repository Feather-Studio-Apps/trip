Everything you need

Built for the way
you actually travel.

From the first dream to the final recap — every part of the journey in one place.

Places

Search and save places to visit. Mark them as you go. Filter by category, city, or favourites.

Budget tracker

Log expenses by category in any currency. See totals, daily averages, and breakdowns at a glance.

Photo journal

Add photos with captions and dates. Link them to specific places. Browse your trip in a polaroid-style album.

Prep & packing

Build checklists and packing lists. Apply reusable templates. Check off as you pack.

World map

Track every country you've visited on an interactive world map. Watch your map fill up over time.

Share & export

Generate a postcard-style trip recap to share. Export trips as files to share with travel companions.
